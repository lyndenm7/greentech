About us

Technology that supports long-term environmental change

GreenTech Solutions was founded to help organizations adopt cleaner systems through smart devices, provide data you can act upon, and to educate you and your business on sustainability.

Our story

GreenTech Solutions began as a startup idea focused on making sustainability more practical for growing businesses. The company blends environmental responsibility with modern digital tools so teams can reduce waste, improve reporting, and build a stronger public commitment to greener operations.
